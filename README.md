# Maple (UofT Food Map)

## Summary
**Discover your next go-to spot near/on campus in seconds!**

You can easily browse through countless options by picking your favourite category or just clicking on a nearby spot on the map.

Not sure if a spot is actually good? Check its community ratings to get more reliable input from fellow students.
